About

The PNT GROUP Ltd. - is a team of several companies specialized in industrial construction services. We offer a wide range of services, including design, construction, reconstruction and repair of buildings and structures, including the production of metal structures.
MISSION OF PNT GROUP
Our Mission - Is to become one of the strongest companies specializing in the construction of industrial facilities, with the best staff of engineering staff and specialists in the field of interior decoration and design.
PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE
Our Clients - Work in all sectors, including agriculture, light industry, manufacturing, construction, real estate, retail and logistics. We take full responsibility for the work -from design to commissioning.
OUR POSSIBILITIES
The PNT GROUP Ltd. - A comprehensive company with its own steel fabrication plant, design office, building materials warehouses, a fleet of specialized equipment and construction teams selected over the years under the guidance of experienced managers.
